Meet Andy

Cool Catch Phrase Here

Andy is a 23 y/o Pasadena ISD alum and Columbia University graduate who has returned to Pasadena, TX, and is currently working for the ISS Program at NASA's Johnson Space Center. He graduated from Pasadena Memorial High School in 2018 as a part of the District's Dual-Language Program, and is fluent in both Spanish and English. Andy is running to advocate for an equitable, rigorous, and fulfilling education for every student the district serves, and to work towards bridging the gap between the community and the school district in Pasadena. He brings in plenty of his own ideas, and is excited to begin meeting folks in the community.

In early 2022, Andy began looking for opportunities in the greater Houston area, hoping to move back to Pasadena post-graduation, with the intent of improving the educational opportunities available to the students here, and therefore the outcomes in this area. In the developing minds of Pasadena students Andy sees reflections of himself, and students who will flourish when guided by leaders who know their struggles and anxieties. Andy has been in their shoes, can speak the two dominant languages of our community, and is determined to engender meaningful and lasting change for the students of Pasadena. It's not about him nor his opponent, rather, it's all about the students.
